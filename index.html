About Us

Harryfarm is a leading manufacturing company based in Yewa South, Ogun State, Nigeria. Located at CDA Mosafejo behind High Court Ilaro, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality manufactured products that meet the needs of Nigerian businesses and consumers.

Our strategic location in Ogun State positions us perfectly to serve clients across the South-West region and beyond. We combine modern manufacturing techniques with local expertise to produce reliable, durable products that stand the test of time.

At Harryfarm, quality control is not just a process - it's our commitment to every client we serve.
